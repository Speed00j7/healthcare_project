About HealthAssist

HealthAssist is an AI-powered health assistant designed to provide users with preliminary health advice based on their symptoms. This project leverages advanced AI models to analyze user input and generate potential health insights.

Features

  • Symptom Analysis: Users can input their symptoms to receive potential health advice.
  • AI-Powered Insights: Utilizes AI models to predict possible diseases and suggest precautions.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy-to-use interface with clear instructions and results.

Disclaimer

HealthAssist is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for accurate diagnosis and treatment options.
